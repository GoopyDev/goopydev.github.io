Special Girl

Special Girl is a game about a young girl with magical powers who is trapped in a room in a secret military base located in a cave, guarded by soldiers. Our heroine can throw balls of elemental powers (ice, fire, and electricity) to make her way. She then begins her escape, leaving behind the military base, and seeks a safe place to hide. Before she can get there, she travels through a fantasy world with trees and green grass, battles with moving monster mushrooms, and eventually arrives at a cabin that will become her home.

For this 3D game created with Unity, I utilized textured terrains, various objects, and characters from the Unity Assets Store, including our heroine, soldiers, trees, and mushrooms. Additionally, we developed scripts in C# for interaction with in-game objects, an energy counter for our character and each enemy, and employed NavMesh to enable character and enemy movement throughout the map.
