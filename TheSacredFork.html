The Sacred Fork

"The Sacred Fork" is a culinary-themed website aimed at providing users with a delightful exploration of food and recipes. Powered by the Spoonacular API, this platform offers a comprehensive database of diverse recipes from various cuisines around the world.

Users can embark on a culinary journey by searching for recipes using keywords, dish names, or specific dietary preferences. The interface allows for easy filtering and sorting of results based on factors like dietary restrictions and health scores.

Additionally, users have the option to create their own recipes, complete with step-by-step instructions and captivating images. With a focus on user experience and accessibility, "The Sacred Fork" strives to inspire and empower individuals to discover the joy of cooking and experimenting with new flavors in the kitchen.

This application was built using JavaScript, Node.js, React, Redux, PostgreSQL, Styled Components, Express, and Sequelize.
