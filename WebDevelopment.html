Full Stack Web Development

During my time at Bootcamp Henry, I embarked on an exciting journey of learning in the world of full-stack web development. Throughout the course, I acquired essential skills that complemented my prior knowledge of HTML, CSS, as well as programming and design.

I immersed myself in technologies such as JavaScript, React, Node, Next, Express, StyledComponents, Tailwind, Bootstrap, and LESS. I learned to create web applications with controlled forms and modularized components, giving a professional and stylized touch to my projects.

I also mastered the creation of servers and the management of requests, enabling me to build dynamic routes for smoother navigation. Validating data received from forms became second nature, and I could respond to requests with appropriate status codes.

One of the most exciting projects was Videogames Blackhole, an extensive video game library powered by the RAWG API, and The Sacred Fork, a cooking recipe website that leveraged the Spoonacular API to search, sort, and filter recipes.

I also took part in an exhilarating group project, ElectroShop, which focused on an online store for gamer items. We created a shopping cart and established a payment process using MercadoPagoSDK. Additionally, we allowed users to create accounts and utilized LocalStorage to maintain session data and the shopping cart. My time at Bootcamp Henry was an intense but incredibly rewarding experience.

These skills complemented my existing knowledge and have prepared me for an exciting future in full-stack web development.
