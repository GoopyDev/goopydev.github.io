La Tanqueta

La Tanqueta is an engaging 3D split-screen player vs. player game where each player controls a tank, aiming to eliminate their opponent amidst a dynamic battlefield filled with destructible obstacles. Victory is achieved by successfully landing three shots on the opponent or by capturing the flag before them. To aid in their mission, players can strategically utilize power-ups scattered throughout the map, including rapid-fire, high-velocity shots, machine guns, increased speed, and health restoration. Control options are versatile, allowing players to navigate their tanks using either keyboard, mouse, or gamepad. The game's intuitive design ensures that tank aiming remains independent of movement direction, providing players with precise control over their firepower.

Built with Unity, La Tanqueta delivers smooth gameplay, powered by effective C# scripts. These scripts govern not only tank movement and aiming mechanics but also dynamically generate unique maps each time a new game begins, ensuring endless replayability and excitement.
