The Duke's Order

Embark on an epic quest in 'The Duke's Order', a captivating 2.5D adventure set in a stunning 3D landscape with 2D characters. As a valiant medieval knight, you're tasked by the duke to hunt down a fearsome dragon terrorizing the realm. Leaving the safety of the castle behind, you traverse lush green hills, engaging in battles against colossal ants and overcoming treacherous obstacles on your journey to the towering mountain where the dragon lairs. Armed with your trusty sword and nimble footwork, you must confront the dragon in its cave, chaining together powerful attacks to emerge victorious.

Crafted with Unity, 'The Duke's Order' features meticulously animated characters using Anima2D, delivering fluid and lifelike movements for an immersive gameplay experience. Unleash devastating combos with up to three strikes, utilizing swift lateral slashes and a piercing thrust. Whether walking or running, your actions are met with dynamic responses from the environment, including the vigilant detection of nearby threats by the relentless ants. Are you ready to heed the duke's call and vanquish the dragon in 'The Duke's Order'?
