ElectroShop

We, as a team, have developed a Gaming Store project. The platform allows you to browse products from four categories (although more products and categories can be added by the administrator user through the built-in dashboard). You can easily add products to your cart, modify quantities, remove items, or empty the cart altogether.

Additionally, the platform provides the option to create an account, which is necessary for completing a purchase. During the purchase process, you will be guided through a brief confirmation step, and the MercadoPagoSDK will lead you to the payment form.

Within the 'User' section, you can access your personal data, view past orders, manage your current cart, and choose the option to log out.

Technologies used in this project include: JavaScript | Next | ReduxToolkit | Express | Sequelize | PostgreSQL | Tailwind | StyledComponents | ReactIcons | Toastify | MercadoPagoSDK
