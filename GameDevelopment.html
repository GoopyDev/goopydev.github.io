Game Development

Studying at Image Campus, I embarked on an exciting learning journey in the fascinating world of video games. This prestigious institution, specialized in game development and design, art, audio, and animation, provided me with a comprehensive education in various aspects of the industry.

The core focus of the course was game development in Unity 3D. I learned to use C# scripts to create game logic, exploring a wide range of techniques and concepts that ranged from scene and camera setup to the creation of both 2D and 3D games.

I mastered collision management and detection, as well as user interaction with keyboards, mice, and gamepads, allowing me to design immersive and dynamic gaming experiences. Additionally, I acquired skills in terrain creation, texture application, incorporation of music and sound effects, character animation using Anima2D, and character navigation with NavMesh.

Lighting and user interface design were also key aspects of my education. Through projects like "La tanqueta," "The Duke's Order," and "Special Girl," I was able to apply these skills in practice and develop impressive demos that showcased my abilities and creativity.
